    Come Get to Know Us!

    Austin Sanderson is a certified pop music enthusiast. Growing up in Rhode Island, he passed the time by memorizing every lyric to Lady Gaga's Born This Way and Kylie Minogue’s Fever. He can spend hours talking about pop culture (especially with Kiara) and decided to make a show about it. While his knowledge of pop music comes solely from Wikipedia and Reddit, he tries his best carry a good conversation about the topic at hand. If you see him on the street, a simple smile and a wave will make his day! He worked very hard on this website and hopes everyone enjoys their time on this page. One thing that the average person does not know about this fellow is favorite movie is Moulin Rouge!

    Kiara Smith is from the humble hometown of Little Rock, Arkansas and yes, she is aware that she is the first person you have ever met from Arkansas. She is a junior Arts and Entertainment Management Major and she loves all thing Pop Culture, but specifically all things Blue Ivy, Beyonce, and the twins Rumi and Sir. Her career goal is to become a creative director for concert tours and to be rich enough to get a fruit tart from whenever she wants. All in all she is a simple girl that likes good music and good people. If you see her in the street she is probably listening to Up Out My Face by Mariah Carey. However, if you can't find her then she is probably in her room listening to HAIM’s Something to Tell You album for the millionth time.
